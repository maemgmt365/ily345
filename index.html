the story

this didn’t start as a brand.

someone once shared the idea of 345 with me through something simple — the more things you say yes to, the more things you have to say no to.

when you’re building something, chasing something, or just locked in, sometimes the people you care about get less of you.
for me, that showed up in missed conversations and not always being present.

so instead of leaving it there — i’d just send 345.

a simple way of saying i’m here, i’m thinking about you, and i still mean these words.

over time, it became more than that.

not just something you send in absence — but something you send anytime.

a habit. a check-in. a sign-off.

the message

love yours.

your life. your people. the ones around you.

a simple way to remind someone what they mean to you — whether it’s a habit, a check-in, or something you just want them to feel.

whether in absence or in abundance, these are words we don’t hear enough.

i love you.
i believe in you.
i am proud of you.
