the story

a reminder to say the things we already feel.

the heart of 345 is simple. there are things we think about the people we care about that do not always get said — not because they are untrue, but because life gets fast, communication gets missed, and reassurance gets delayed.

3 means i love you.
4 means i believe in you.
5 means i am proud of you.
something small can still say something real. that is the point.

345 was built as a way to share those words when you have not been able to, when someone needs reassurance, or when you simply want them to know they are on your mind.

it is for the friend who needs encouragement, the family member you have not checked on enough, the partner you want to reassure, or the person who rarely hears these words at all.

the goal is not to overcomplicate it. the goal is to make the message easier to carry, easier to give, and harder to leave unsaid.
